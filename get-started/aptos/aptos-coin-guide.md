# Get Aptos Coins

![](../.gitbook/images/aptos-header.png)

The native token of Aptos blockchain is **Aptos Coin (APT)**.

To do most things on Aptos blockchain, you will need to pay gas, which comes in the form of APT.

You will also need the tokens you want to trade or stake on Aptos blockchain. There are a few ways to bring your assets to Aptos blockchain, but here are the ones we recommend:
