# Get BEP20 Tokens

![](../.gitbook/images/bep20-tokens-header.png)

**BEP20** is one of BNB Smart Chain's native tokens.

Gas, which is paid for with BEP20 BNB, is required for the majority of tasks on BSC.

A BEP20 version of any token you want to trade, stake, etc. on BSC is also required.

There are several ways to convert your tokens to BEP20, however the following are the ones we advise:
